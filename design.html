Design

  • We will meet with you on site at a time that works for you
  • We will identify your requirements and find solutions for your ideas
  • After a measured and photographic survey of your property we will implement these ideas and create existing and proposed drawings for your review
  • We will create up to two draft proposed floor plans and elevations layout options scaled at 1:50 or 1:100 for your review and comments
  • We will make amendments accordingly
  • If required we will propose a sketch concept of the external proposed extension / conversion
  • Based on your comments, your selected option will be revised and developed into proposed floor plans and elevations at either scale 1:50 or 1:100
